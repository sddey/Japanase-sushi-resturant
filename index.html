Our Best 3 Popular Dishes

Nigiri Sushi

Nigiri sushi is a classic and widely recognized type of sushi. It consists of a small mound of vinegared rice topped with a slice of raw or cooked seafood, such as tuna (maguro), salmon (sake), or shrimp (ebi)

Maki Sushi

Maki sushi refers to sushi rolls that are wrapped in seaweed (nori) with various ingredients. The most well-known type of maki sushi is the California Roll, which typically includes crab or imitation crab, avocado, and cucumber

Sashimi

While not technically sushi (which includes rice), sashimi is a popular Japanese dish consisting of thinly sliced, raw seafood or fish served without rice.
